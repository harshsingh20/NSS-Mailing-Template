To succeed you have to lead. You have to do what's best for the people; you must bring the best out of your ability and capability.” That is exactly what we do in NSS. National Service Scheme provides diversified opportunities to students in colleges and Universities to develop their personalities through community services. The NSS Unit of Medi-Caps University undergoes the recruitment process for new volunteers every year.

Why Join NSS?

  • NSS is the only unit where you get the opportunity to go on trips and excursions.No other clubs in the university offers this opportunity
  • It focuses on overall development of personality and character of the youth through voluntary community service by participating in events like blood donation drive, plantation drive, vaccination, water conservation, book donation.
  • It is a one time opportunity for everyone where you go as a group of friends and come back as nothing less than a family.  
  • After completing 3 year course of nss, you get a government authorized certificate which gets you recession in govt jobs too.
